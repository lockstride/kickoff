# Business Plan: PayFlow

## Executive Summary

PayFlow is building real-time B2B payment infrastructure for small and medium businesses. We solve the 30-90 day payment delay that causes 82% of SMB failures. Our instant settlement platform integrates with existing accounting software, requiring no workflow changes.

**Funding ask**: $2M seed for 18-month runway
**Use of funds**: Engineering (60%), Go-to-market (25%), Operations (15%)

## Problem & Opportunity

### The Problem

B2B payments are broken for SMBs:
- Average invoice takes 45 days to settle
- 82% of SMB failures cite cash flow as primary cause
- Short-term financing costs 18-24% APR
- 6.1M US businesses affected in our target segment

### Market Opportunity

- **TAM**: $1.2T annual B2B payments in SMB segment
- **SAM**: $120B addressable with current product
- **SOM**: $6B realistic 5-year capture (5% penetration)

### Why Now

- Real-time payment rails (RTP, FedNow) now available
- Open banking APIs enable seamless integration
- COVID accelerated digital payment adoption
- Incumbents focused on enterprise, ignoring SMB

## Solution

PayFlow provides three core capabilities:

1. **Instant Settlement**: Invoices paid in seconds, not weeks
2. **Smart Reconciliation**: Automatic payment-to-invoice matching
3. **Cash Flow Visibility**: Real-time receivables/payables dashboard

### Competitive Advantage

| Competitor | Weakness | PayFlow Advantage |
|------------|----------|-------------------|
| Bill.com | Slow settlement (3-5 days) | Instant settlement |
| Stripe | Developer-focused, complex | No-code SMB setup |
| Banks | Expensive, slow | 10x faster, 80% cheaper |

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

1. **Transaction fees**: 0.5% per instant settlement
2. **SaaS subscription**: $99/month for dashboard + integrations
3. **Float revenue**: Interest on funds in transit

### Unit Economics

- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)**: $450
- **Monthly Revenue per Customer**: $180 (avg)
- **Gross Margin**: 75%
- **LTV**: $2,160 (12-month avg lifetime)
- **LTV:CAC**: 4.8x
- **Payback Period**: 2.5 months

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Wedge (Months 1-6)
- Target: Professional services firms (agencies, consultancies)
- Channel: Direct outreach, accounting firm partnerships
- Goal: 100 paying customers

### Phase 2: Expand (Months 7-12)
- Target: Broader SMB segment
- Channel: Content marketing, referral program
- Goal: 500 paying customers

### Phase 3: Scale (Months 13-18)
- Target: Mid-market expansion
- Channel: Partner integrations, inside sales
- Goal: 2,000 paying customers
